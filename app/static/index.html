Automate your group meetings schedule

Schedr helps schedule meeting based on each members' free time. Fast.


How it works:

Setup

The admin indicates customisable time range for meetings and alert each member to indicate their free timings.


Schedule

Schedr automatically find the best meeting time based on each member's calender. No effort on your side.


Reschedule

Needs to reschedule? No worries, Schedr will help you find the next appropriate timing and alert members.
